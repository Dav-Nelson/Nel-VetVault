About Nel VetVault

Hi, I'm Nelson, a final-year veterinary Medicine student of University of Jos.

I created Nel VetVault out of a genuine desire to make studying easier, faster, and more organized for fellow Vet Medicine students. I understand how overwhelming it can be to find and organize study resources- because I've been there too.

This platform gives you quick access to past questions, lecture notes, and helpful visuals- all neatly structured by level and courses.

I hope you use it actively, share it with others, and even contribute to help grow the vault. Together, we can make veterinary knowledge more accessible.
